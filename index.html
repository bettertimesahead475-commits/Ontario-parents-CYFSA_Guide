Verified ways to find help (no fake listings)

This site links you to official directories so you can verify any lawyer’s license and contact details yourself.

Official application steps + eligibility screening.
2
LAO Contact Centre
Toll-free contact details + hours.
3
Law Society of Ontario (LSO) Lawyer Directory
Verify license status, practice restrictions, and displayed contact info.
4
Law Society Referral Service (LSRS)
Get a referral for a short consultation (LSO-operated).
Do not trust random “lawyer lists”. Only use verified directories (LSO / LAO / LSRS). If you add firm listings to this site, link to the firm’s official site and keep screenshots of verification.
